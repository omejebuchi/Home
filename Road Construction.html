Services

Our Services range from construction of federal, state and local roads for the government to development of world class estates for individuals. The Tropical is known for its effectiveness and timely delivery of contracts

ROAD CONSTRUCTION

Our projects involve the construction of asphaltic and surface dressed roads, the expansion and rehabilitation of existing roads, infrastructure projects and airport runways in various States across Nigeria. To date The Tropical has successfully constructed and delivered 250 Kilometers of new roads and carriage ways, and has rehabilitated 125 Kilometers of previously constructed roads.
